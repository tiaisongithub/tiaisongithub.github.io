When we started this project, we decided to go for something simple. We chose to make a flickering light that turns on and off on a constant pattern. One of the main complications that we had with the project was the wiring. Initially, the light would not turn on at all. Then, we rewired the board, and we found out that one of the wires was broken. Afterwards, the light is able to turn on. Regardless, the light would not blink. After attempting several sample codes in the audrino software, we figured it out and the light was blinking. Khalil wired the board, and I ended up finding the right code to enable the light to blink and work as intended. Another problem that we confronted involved a button. We tried to program the button to control the light blinking, but we decided to let the light flicker on its own.
